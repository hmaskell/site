The Client

Golden Ears Cheesecrafters are an award-winning artisan cheesecrafting family business. They sell their cheeses and other products in their farm store in Maple Ridge and at local Farmers Markets, run a restaurant with reservation-only dinner events, and offer cooking classes.

The Problem

Previously, they had been selling their services across 3 separate sites: one for e-Commerce, one for event booking, and one for information.

The Goal

Craft a website that unifies product sales, booking and information into one cohesive, on-brand experience.
