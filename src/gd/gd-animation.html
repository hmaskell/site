Motion Design

Personal projects I've worked on as a creative outlet and excuse to brush up on motion design and illustration.

  • Drug Store

    Drawn and animated pixel by pixel in Photoshop by reference from a photo I took of my local Rite Aid one evening in the snow. Inspired by Mark Ferrari's 8-bitish artwork for Thimbleweed Park.

  • Sk8-Bit Series

    Created using Piskel, a free online pixel art tool, using videos of skateboarders as reference for motion.

  • ~!@shoes

    Concept exploration through frame-by-frame animation, drawn and animated with Rough Animator for iPad

  • Punisher - Phoebe Bridgers

    Fan art for an Indie album drawn and animated with Rough Animator for iPad

  • Kinetic Typography - Shaun of the Dead

    Assets created in Adobe Illustrated, animated in After Effects. Sound clip from Shaun of the Dead.
